Featured Locations

  • Burgas

    Burgas is the fourth biggest city, and biggest area in Bulgaria. It is also one of the biggest ports in Entire Europe! Awesome resorts make this place really beautiful and worthy to visit.

  • Veliko Tarnovo

    Former Bulgarian Capital, and one of the most history-rich and oldest cities in Eastern Europe. Tarnovo, as it is sometimes refferred to, is also a host of a lot of traditional beer factories and brasseries.

  • Plovdiv

    An ancient Thracian province capital and modern administrative centre, Plovdiv never stops growing and glowing from it's bright future ahead. If you want to visit this city, you should prepare for a lot of ancient history.

  • Vidin

    Vidin lies on the banks of the Danube River, which combined with it's close proximity with Romania and Serbia, gave it a big trading importance, therefore turning the city into a prospering marketplace. Nowadays, people can see a lot of old fortresses.
